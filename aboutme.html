About Me

Hi! I’m Benjamin Smith, a tennis instructor in Rexburg who loves helping players build solid fundamentals and confidence on the court.

I’m a lifelong tennis player with a passion for coaching. I’ve played competitively and recreationalally for years, and now I focus on helping others develop both their skills and love for the game.

I have several years of experience teaching players of all ages—from young beginners learning proper grip and footwork to advanced athletes refining their technique, consistency, and strategy.

I started coaching after helping a friend improve his serve. Seeing his excitement as he improved made me want to teach others. Since then, tennis instruction has become one of my greatest joys.
