Welcome to Basline Tennis

Welcome to Baseline Tennis—I’m Benjamin Smith, and I’m here to help players of all ages and skill levels grow their game. Whether you’re picking up a racket for the first time, returning after a break, or looking to refine your technique, my lessons are designed to meet you exactly where you are. I focus on clear instruction, practical skill-building, and creating a positive environment where anyone can feel confident on the court. No matter your experience, I’m committed to helping you improve, have fun, and enjoy everything tennis has to offer.
